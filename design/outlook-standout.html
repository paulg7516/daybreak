Recommendation

Move the brand off blue to a warm dawn color. Sunrise coral (#FF6A3D) is the strongest: it owns the "daybreak" idea, has the highest contrast against Outlook's blue chrome, and no Microsoft tool looks like it. If you want to keep maximum daylight between the brand and the in-app red/amber urgency badges, Dawn rose (#FB5673) is the close runner-up. Violet is the safe-but-generic fallback. Whichever wins, it carries the whole system: site accent, app primary, and the Outlook add-in icon.
